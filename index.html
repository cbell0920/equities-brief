But underneath those headline numbers, advancing shares outnumbered declining ones every day. Money didn't leave the market. It moved from tech to healthcare, industrials, and financials. That's a healthy bull market rotation, not the beginning of a bear market.

Comcast is today's headline trade. The NBCUniversal and Sky spinoff is a genuine value-unlock event — not a financial engineering gimmick, but a structural separation that gives each business an entrepreneurial management team and access to dedicated capital. If the gap holds above prior close, the trade is valid. If it fades hard, step aside.
