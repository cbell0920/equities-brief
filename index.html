Senior Analyst Note
TPTraders Senior Equities Analyst — June 30, 2026

The market is closing Q2 with a powerful statement: the AI cost concerns and Middle East jitters that dominated June were sentiment-driven, not fundamental. Monday's 2.45% Nasdaq-100 surge — the strongest single session in weeks — happened the moment geopolitical risk eased. Chip stocks that fell 7.9% as a sector last week are already rebounding. This pattern, fear-driven selloff followed by sharp recovery once the catalyst resolves, is the hallmark of a market with strong underlying fundamentals getting temporarily knocked off balance by external shocks.

Today's standout trade is Rocket Lab. The $8 billion Iridium acquisition isn't just a pop on news — it's a structural repositioning of the space services competitive landscape. Roth Capital's immediate price target increase reflects genuine analyst conviction, not knee-jerk momentum chasing. The acquisition gives Rocket Lab a more formidable position against SpaceX's growing dominance. This is the kind of M&A-driven catalyst that can sustain a multi-day move if the market continues digesting the strategic logic positively.

SanDisk's Bernstein upgrade deserves attention precisely because of its magnitude. A price target increase from $1,700 to $3,000 — nearly 76% implied upside — from a respected sell-side shop is not a casual call. It signals genuine institutional conviction that the NAND memory cycle has more room to run than consensus currently believes. Combined with Micron's blowout quarter and SanDisk's own position as the S&P 500's best 2026 performer, this sector deserves continued attention even after the recent run.

Caution flag: Bank of America's three-wave correction warning shouldn't be dismissed. Markets rarely move in straight lines, and a quarter that started with semiconductor euphoria and ended with AI cost anxiety has shown real volatility beneath the surface calm. Today's relief rally is genuine, but discipline matters — don't chase gaps blindly. Use the Doha talks and Thursday's NFP as your guideposts. If both resolve favorably, the rally likely extends into July. If either disappoints, be ready to take profits quickly rather than holding through a reversal.
